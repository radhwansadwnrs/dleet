User Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Log into your Facebook account.
  2. Go to the Settings & Privacy section.
  3. Select "Apps and Websites".
  4. Find and select our app (OS-ADAM).
  5. Click "Remove" to delete all data associated with our app.

For further assistance, please contact us at support@example.com.
